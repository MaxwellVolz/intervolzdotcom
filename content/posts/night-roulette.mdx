Bing Bang Night Roulette

Can’t decide what to do tonight? Night Roulette suggests nearby spots, lets you swipe to curate a shortlist, then “Share a Spin” with friends to pick the place.

1. Get suggested places nearby - like doordash but for local bars/scenes
2. Curate your selection - swipe left or right on places in expanding area
3. Friends - "Share a Spin" - make a roulette with your liked nearby places and have the app decide
